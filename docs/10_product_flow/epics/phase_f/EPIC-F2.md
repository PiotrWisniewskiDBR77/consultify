# EPIC-F2: AI as Facilitator of Difference

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Epic ID** | EPIC-F2 |
| **Phase** | F — Team Expansion |
| **User State** | `TEAM` |
| **Priority** | HIGH |

---

## Intent

AI **surfaces disagreement**, doesn't hide it.

---

## System Requirements

### MUST
- [ ] **Surface disagreement** between perspectives
- [ ] **Synthesize**, not judge
- [ ] Present differences as valuable

### MUST NOT
- [ ] Resolve conflicts artificially
- [ ] Hide disagreements
- [ ] Take sides

---

## AI Mode

```
MODE: FACILITATOR
ALLOWED: surface, compare, synthesize, highlight tension
BLOCKED: judge, resolve, dismiss, favor
```

---

## Acceptance Criteria

| Criteria | Pass Condition |
|----------|----------------|
| Disagreement is **visible** | ✅ |
| AI remains **neutral** | ✅ |
| Synthesis adds **value** | ✅ |
